Realms and Rides

Wizards Realm

The Wizards Realm features our world-famous Castle and wizard town. The surprises in this wizardry world are never ending, from dragon displays to flying wizards,

and you can of course enjoy rides like the tallest tower free fall ride in the world that will definitely take your breath away.

Fairies Realm

The Fairies Realm, will make all your fairytale dreams come true! In the fairy city where you can enjoy the most fairy-like snacks and food and buy the most magical gifts!

Every day at noon you will be able to enjoy the Fairies parade around the city and you can of course enjoy our fairy rides, like the dreamiest carousel you have ever been on.

Elves Realm

The Elves Realm takes you in the heart of the Elves' mystical forest for an experience you will not forget.

The Elves Pagode ride promises an aerial view of the Park you will surely not forget and the Elvish rollercoaster will give you the best tour of the forest and into the nearby lake, for a little splash!

Dwarves Realm

The Dwarves Realm welcomes you to the mighty Dwarves' kingdom, where you can enjoy the only ride in the world that can take you underground,

straight to the heart of the Dwarves' mines and the most fun and colourful rollercoaster that you have ever been on.
